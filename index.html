Get to know me!

Hello! I'm Reginaldo Junior ,a detail-oriented Software QA Analyst with a solid foundation in technology and software development.
My journey into tech comes after a successful 10+ year career in business management, where I honed my skills in data analysis, process optimization, and understanding business needs from the ground up. I decided to pivot my career to technology, driven by a passion for ensuring that software is not only functional but also reliable and valuable to the end-user.
Today, I combine my business acumen with hands-on technical skills in manual and automated testing. My core focus is on Quality Assurance, where I apply my analytical mindset to design comprehensive test plans, identify critical bugs, and collaborate with development teams to enhance product quality. While I am proficient in web technologies like HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Node.Js and SQL. I use this knowledge to write more effective and robust automated tests in frameworks like Cypress. I am passionate about continuous learning and am always exploring new tools and methodologies to improve quality processes. I'm currently seeking a full-time opportunity where I can contribute to building high-quality software. Feel free to connect with me on Linkedin or check out my projects!
